## Quick answer

If you are asking "How much can I spend today?", your bank balance is usually the wrong default. Pip answers with Spendable Cash Today.

## The real question is daily

People rarely open a money app because they want a lecture. They open it because they are about to make a choice.

Can I get lunch out? Can I buy this now? Should I wait? Those are daily questions, not monthly reports.

## Pip gives the daily answer first

Spendable Cash Today is meant to be the first thing you see. It is a starting point for the next decision.

If you want more detail, Pip can explain what changed or show the consequence of a purchase.

## The answer should be calm

Money tools can make every choice feel loaded. Pip tries to keep the answer short, concrete, and low-pressure.
